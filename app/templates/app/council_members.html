Council Members

The Scholar Indexing Society Council members are a group Of international scientists, researchers, and librarians who represent the major scientific disciplines. The members are responsible for reviewing all submissions that are suggested for the Scholar Indexing Society database. This council's members carry out their duties happily, meritoriously, professionally, and quickly to meet the demands of users and clients.
